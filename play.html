Avery snips a play

Moonshine by Arthur Hopkins

LUKE.

Couldn't you pick a fight?

REVENUE.

I'm coming to that. You know up North they believe that a man can be killed in the South for calling another man a liar.

LUKE.

That's right.

REVENUE.

It is, is it? Well, I've called men liars from Washington to Atlanta, and I'm here to tell you about it.

LUKE.

They must a took pity on ye.

REVENUE.

Do you know Two Gun Jake that keeps the dive down in Henderson?

LUKE.

I should think I do.... Jake's killed enough of 'em.
